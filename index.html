About Us

Team Unity Ghana is a gospel music collective headquartered in Ghana, dedicated to spreading the message of Christ through contemporary sounds rooted in Highlife, Afrobeats, choral traditions, and heartfelt worship. We write, arrange, and curate music that points hearts to Jesus while celebrating Ghanaian culture.

The team was founded by Anim Antwi Henry — a Web Developer, IT Consultant, Music Director, and Graphic Designer — who leads with a spirit of excellence across both ministry and media. Under his leadership, our creative process blends Scripture, prayer, musicianship, and modern production to create powerful worship encounters online and on stage.

What we do includes live worship sessions, outreach events, music workshops, media production, and collaborations with churches and creatives across Ghana. We also mentor young ministers and build platforms for emerging gospel talent to grow.

  • Worship: Christ-centered music and prayer that lift faith.
  • Excellence: Quality in sound, visuals, and ministry delivery.
  • Discipleship: Mentorship and training for the next generation.
  • Community: Serving people and partnering with local churches.
  • Innovation: Leveraging design and technology to reach more souls.
